## Project Overview  
Created an interactive **Power BI dashboard** analyzing **credit card performance**, highlighting KPIs such as **customers, transactions, delinquency, revenue, satisfaction, and utilization ratio**.  
The dashboard is structured into **demographics, revenue, and risk insights**, helping businesses monitor performance trends and make data-driven decisions.

## Problem Statement  
**The objective of this project is to analyze and monitor overall credit card performance by tracking customer demographics, transaction activities, revenue, and risk indicators.**  
This helps financial institutions understand portfolio health, customer behavior, and profitability trends.

## Data Used  
- **Data** – Credit card dataset containing customer demographics, transaction history, and account information.  
- **Data Preparation** – Microsoft Excel  
- **Data Modeling & DAX Functions** – Power BI  
- **Data Visualization & Dashboard** – Power BI  

## KPI’s Requirement  
1. **Total Customers** – Measure the number of active customers.  
2. **Transaction Volume** – Track the total number of credit card transactions.  
3. **Transaction Amount** – Analyze total spending to evaluate financial performance.  
4. **Delinquent Accounts** – Monitor overdue accounts to assess credit risk.  
5. **Interest Earned** – Evaluate profitability generated from credit utilization.  
6. **Annual Fees** – Track revenue generated through card fees.  
7. **Customer Satisfaction** – Measure overall customer experience.  
8. **Utilization Ratio** – Assess how effectively credit limits are being used.  

## Charts Requirement  
1. **Customers by State (Map/Chart):** Visualize customer distribution across regions.  
2. **Customers by Occupation (Bar Chart):** Understand customer segmentation by job type.  
3. **Customers by Education (Bar Chart):** Explore correlations between education level and card usage.  
4. **Customers by Marital Status (Pie Chart):** Identify spending patterns based on marital status.  
5. **Transactions and Revenue Overview (Cards/Bar Chart):** Summarize key financial KPIs such as transaction amount, interest earned, and annual fees.  

## Outcomes  
**This dashboard enables financial institutions to:**  
- Monitor credit card performance across multiple customer segments.  
- Identify risk factors such as delinquency and utilization trends.  
- Evaluate customer satisfaction and behavior patterns.  
- Gain actionable insights for revenue optimization and risk reduction.  

> **Note:** This description presents only some of the main highlights; the complete project included deeper analytical processes, multiple iterations, and continuous enhancements aimed at improving usability, visual clarity, and decision-making impact.
